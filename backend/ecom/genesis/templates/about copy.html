Our Story

From streetwear enthusiasts to urban fashion pioneers

Who We Are

Genesis Hoodie District

Founded in 2015, Genesis began as a small collective of designers and streetwear enthusiasts with a shared vision: to create premium hoodies that blend comfort, quality, and urban style. Today, we've grown into a recognized name in contemporary streetwear, staying true to our roots while constantly innovating.

Our Journey

What started in a small Brooklyn studio has grown into a movement. Our first collection featured just five hoodie designs, each handmade and screen-printed by our founders. The response was overwhelming, and we quickly realized we'd tapped into something special.

As demand grew, so did our team - but never our standards. Every Genesis hoodie still receives the same attention to detail as those first pieces. We've expanded our offerings but stayed true to our core philosophy: premium materials, innovative designs, and uncompromising quality.

Today, Genesis hoodies are worn by artists, creators, and urban explorers around the world - people who value both style and substance.
